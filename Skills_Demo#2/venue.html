VENUES







Carton House

If your dream day is all about old world luxury, this historic country manor just outside of Maynooth is definitely one to check out. Expansive and versatile, Carton House is home to lots of wow factor spaces. The Carton Suite Ballroom is your ceremony space for parties of up to 300 guests, while the elegant Silver Ballroom caters for the same number for dinner and dancing.

Marker Hotel

You may know The Marker Hotel in Dublin as a fun spot for brunch or cocktails, but it's also a contemporary, and entirely versatile wedding venue. Expect gasp-worthy interiors, beautiful views of Dublin city and the Irish Sea, a buzzy atmosphere and plenty of boutique accommodation for your guests. Wedding parties of up to 240 guests can be accommodated in the venue's largest space, the chic and minimal Marker Suite.

The K Club

The grand interior spaces and meticulously-groomed gardens at The K Club regularly play host to epic wedding ceremonies, the largest of which can be accommodated in The Legacy Suite ballroom. Located in the Smurfit Clubhouse and newly decorated in the style of the main house, it features French doors leading out to a beautiful terrace, offering knockout views of the lake.
